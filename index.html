Functional Safety · Cybersecurity · SOTIF · AI Safety

One item.
Four disciplines of defense.

FuCSAI is a browser-based workspace covering:

  1. Automotive Functional Safety as per ISO 26262
  2. Automotive Cybersecurity Engineering according to ISO/SAE 21434 & UN ECE Regulations
  3. Safety of the Intended Function (SOTIF) as per ISO 21448
  4. Automotive Safety and Artificial Intelligence as per ISO/PAS 8800

What FuCSAI covers

Four disciplines, one traceable model

Each layer defends the same item against a different class of failure — and in FuCSAI they share the same underlying data, so a change in one shows up everywhere it matters.

Fu
Functional Safety
ISO 26262
Fault-based hazard analysis and risk reduction across the full V-model.
Work products
  • Item Definition
  • Hazard Analysis & Risk Assessment (HARA)
  • Safety Goals & Functional Safety Concept
  • Technical Safety Concept
  • FMEA / FTA
  • FMEDA & Diagnostic Coverage
  • Dependent Failure Analysis
  • Safety Case
C
Cybersecurity
ISO/SAE 21434
Threat-based risk assessment and treatment across the product lifecycle.
Work products
  • Item Definition & Asset Identification
  • Threat Analysis & Risk Assessment (TARA)
  • Attack Path Analysis & Feasibility Rating
  • Cybersecurity Goals
  • Cybersecurity Concept & Specifications
  • Vulnerability Analysis
  • Cybersecurity Case
  • CSMS evidence (UN R155/R156)
S
SOTIF
ISO 21448
Performance-limitation and foreseeable-misuse risk beyond fault-based hazards.
Work products
  • Functional & Sensor Specification
  • Identification of Triggering Conditions
  • Scenario-Based Hazard Analysis
  • Known/Unknown Scenario Evaluation
  • Verification & Validation Targets
  • SOTIF Evaluation Report
  • Field Monitoring Plan
AI
AI Safety
ISO/PAS 8800
Data, model, and behavioral risk for learned components, extending ISO 26262 & ISO 21448.
Work products
  • AI Element Definition
  • AI Safety Risk Assessment
  • Dataset Requirements & Data Management
  • AI Safety Requirements
  • AI Model V&V (test, validate)
  • Safety Analysis for AI Elements
  • AI Assurance Argument
  • Post-Deployment Monitoring
